Arroz Con Pollo

Delicious rice and chicken recipe for a cozy dinner

This recipe is an easy weeknight dish to prepare and packed with flavor and spices.


Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large green pepper, chopped into small cubes
  • 1 large red pepper, chopped into small cubes
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1-2 scallion stalks, finely chopped
  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 packet of seasoning Sazon Goya con Azafran
  • 2 cups (half a packet) frozen mixed vegetables
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Goya Adobo

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with soy sauce, salt, pepper, and adobo in a mixing bowl and set aside (preferably do this at least a couple hours before cooking)
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Saut√© onion, peppers, garlic and green onion for 3-5 minutes, or until onion is translucent. Season with salt/pepper/adobo as needed. Remove from pan and reserve.
  3. In the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon of oil and cook the chicken for 8 to 10 minutes or until chicken is lightly browned. Remove from the pan and reserve. Remove any excess liquid as well with the chicken.
  4. In the same pan, add 1 tablespoon of oil. Add the rice and toast for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  5. Add chicken broth, onion mixture, cooked chicken, saffron (or seasoning) and mix well.
  6. When the saffron is well blended, add the vegetables; mix. Cook, over medium-high heat, until most of the liquid has evaporated.
  7. When the liquid has evaporated cover, reduce heat to low and cook for 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  8. If there is still liquid, leave cover ajar so it can boil off
  9. Taste test and add additional seasoning if necessary
  10. Serve hot
